How We Protect You

Misleading Actions

Flags buttons and links that trick you into unintended actions like subscriptions or purchases.

Fake Urgency

Identifies false countdowns and limited-time offers designed to pressure you into quick decisions.

Pre-selected Options

Alerts you about pre-checked boxes that sign you up for newsletters or additional charges.

Trick Questions

Highlights confusing wording designed to make you agree to things you didn't intend to.

Installation Guide

1

Download the Extension

Clone the repository or download it from GitHub:

git clone https://github.com/SunilSuthar7/webshield-ai.git
2

Load in Chrome

1. Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions/
2. Enable "Developer mode" in the top right
3. Click "Load unpacked" and select the extension folder

3

Configure Settings

1. Click the extension icon in your browser
2. Customize your protection settings
3. Start browsing with confidence!

About WebSHIELD.AI

WebSHIELD.AI is an open-source browser extension dedicated to protecting users from deceptive dark patterns and manipulative UI tactics. Our mission is to empower users to browse the web with confidence and transparency.

Our team is passionate about digital rights and user empowerment. We believe everyone deserves a safe, honest, and transparent online experience. WebSHIELD.AI leverages advanced detection algorithms to identify and block manipulative design practices, ensuring you are always in control of your choices.

Whether you are shopping, signing up for services, or simply browsing, WebSHIELD.AI works silently in the background to protect you from misleading actions, fake urgency, pre-selected options, and trick questions. Join our community and help us make the web a better place for everyone!
